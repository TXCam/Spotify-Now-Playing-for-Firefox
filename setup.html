One-Time Setup

Follow these steps to connect the extension to Spotify.

1

Go to the Spotify Developer Dashboard and log in.

2

Click CREATE APP. Give it any name and description (e.g., "Sidebar Extension").

3

After creating the app, you will see your Client ID. Copy it and paste it in the input box below.

4

Now, click EDIT SETTINGS on your app's page in the dashboard.

5

Find the Redirect URIs section. Copy the exact URL below and add it to the list, then click SAVE at the bottom of the Spotify page.
